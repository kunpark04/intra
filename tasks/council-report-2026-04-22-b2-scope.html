LLM Council Verdict — B2 Scope & Sequencing

Question: Within Path B (broaden evidence base, no deployment of combo-865), which B2 candidate(s) to advance, how to sequence vs B1, how to account for Stage-0 multiplicity, how to treat combo-1298's rank-1 Sharpe, and whether to inherit Probe 2's exact gate.

The Recommendation

Run combo-1298 + combo-664 in parallel on 1h held-out, with per-session decomposition baked into the preregistration. Drop combo-1149. Gate on a relative-Sharpe test (1298 − 664) as primary, absolute gates as secondary. Do not sequence B2 before B1 unconditionally — make B2's design produce the session-structure evidence that would otherwise require B1.

Combo-664 is not a negative control — it is the treatment. Under H0 ("cross-TF coherence is a Stage-1 overfit"), 664 and 1298 pass at equal rates. Under H1 ("coherence identifies genuine edge"), 664 should systematically underperform. This is the only design where H0 and H1 make different predictions.

Combo-1298's rank-1-of-3000 Sharpe is an H0 discount, not an H1 boost. After the V3/V4 ML#2 stacks dissolved under combo-agnostic audit on 2026-04-21, "literal extremum of a sweep" is the project's own base rate failure mode.

The One Thing to Do First

Write the B2 preregistration now with per-session decomposition (SES_2 overnight / SES_0 all / SES_1 RTH) and a relative-Sharpe primary gate (1298 − 664 ≥ 1.3 on ≥50 trades each on 1h held-out). Sign it, commit it, push to sweep-runner-1 before running a single backtest.

The session decomposition is the non-negotiable addition every advisor missed and every reviewer caught — without it, B2 cannot distinguish combo generalization from GLOBEX session inheritance, and you will have spent another probe cycle re-measuring what Probe 3 already found.

Where the Council Clashes

Include 664 or drop it?

Contrarian, First Principles, Outsider: include (as treatment). Executor: drop (doubles writeup burden; 865 @ 15m already serves as control). Expansionist: include but as control, not treatment. The Executor's substitution is weak — 15m failure is a timeframe-axis result from Probe 2, not a test of the Stage-0 selection property.

Gate: absolute or relative?

First Principles: relative-Sharpe (1298 − 664) dissolves multiplicity and is the only falsifiable design. Executor: absolute with Bonferroni-inflated threshold (1.4 instead of 1.3). Contrarian: keep Probe 2's 1.3 exactly. This is the sharpest methodological disagreement.

1149 in or out?

Expansionist and Executor keep it. First Principles drops it (distant params = separate hypothesis, contaminates design). Contrarian drops it (101 trades will fail regime-halves sub-gate). On the evidence, 1149 is dead weight — 101 trades cannot survive a Probe-3-style halves gate.

B2 vs B1 sequencing — the real clash, surfaced by reviewers

Every advisor said B2 → B1. All five reviewers independently flagged that none of the advisors asked whether 1298/1149/664's 1h edge concentrates overnight the way 865's does (3.45× per-trade net ratio). If it does, B2 is re-measuring one phenomenon (the GLOBEX session effect) three times under different combo labels. In that world, B1 (session-structure sweep) is logically prior to B2 — reversing the entire sequencing plan.

Blind Spots the Council Caught

The overnight-GLOBEX confound. Unanimous among reviewers, invisible to all five advisors. Probe 3 established 865's edge is ~3.45× overnight vs RTH. If 1298 and 664 share that session structure, any B2 "pass" measures session-timing, not combo-level or property-level edge. Decision-changing.

1298 Δ=1 param-neighbor of 865 means "same basin, one observation." Two points in one microstructure basin is not two independent pieces of evidence.

Rank-1 conditional on a reverse-engineered filter is a construction extremum. Even Bonferroni α/3 understates the real multiplicity because the 3-member shortlist was generated by filtering 3000 with a property defined by 865.

Probe 1 §7.6 terminality. A second 1h singleton does not re-open the family-level falsification. N_1.3 ≥ 10 is the gate; 2 ≠ 10. Expansionist's "fractures the sunset narrative" framing was wrong.

Advisor Responses

The Contrarian

The shortlist is a trap. Stage-0 multiplicity is already a lie — you screened the entire 3000-combo sweep for cross-TF coherence, found 3, now want robustness on survivors. The cross-TF coherence property has no preregistered evidentiary weight; it was reverse-engineered from 865's success.

Combo-1298 is the most dangerous, not safest. Rank 1 of 3000 is an extremum. Its microstructure Δ=1 neighborhood of 865 means they're the same basin — a basin-wide backtest artifact explains both. P(PASS|H0) on 1298 is HIGHER than random rank-500.

1149 is a fig leaf (101 trades; regime-halves sub-gate will fail on thin tails). 664 as negative control is the only intellectually honest entry.

Answer: (D) 664 alone, Probe 2 gate unchanged. If 664 passes despite rank-6, gate is broken and 865 was luck. Don't advance 1298 — can't out-audit rank-1 extremum. B2 before B1.

The First Principles Thinker

You're asking the wrong question. Real question: what would B2 evidence actually prove? H1 as framed is mush. Cross-TF coherence is Stage-1-selected; passing 1298 corroborates the selection procedure, not 865. Two survivors from a property-filtered triplet is autocorrelated evidence dressed up.

Correct question: what minimal experiment distinguishes "coherence property identifies genuine edge" from "property is itself overfit"? That experiment is 664 — the pre-gate failure. Under H0, 664 passes OOS as likely as 1298. Under H1, 664 underperforms. 664 is the ONLY candidate whose result carries information.

Reframe: Run 1298 AND 664 together; 664 is TREATMENT not control. Drop 1149 (distant params = separate hypothesis, contaminates design). Gate on RELATIVE performance (1298 minus 664 net Sharpe), not absolute thresholds.

Stop optimizing for PASS. Optimize for falsifiability.

The Expansionist

The asymmetry nobody is pricing: combo-1298 is not a "second candidate" — it's a potential family-level unfalsification event. Probe 1 said N_1.3(1h) = 4. If 1298 passes held-out, we have two independent 1h survivors with cross-TF coherence, and Probe 1 Branch A sunset narrative fractures.

Q1: Run B (1298 + 1149 parallel). Include 664 as negative control — pre-gate-fail passing held-out would signal pre-gate is miscalibrated. Q2: B2 first — if 1298 passes, B1 gets a second anchor and becomes generalization probe, not rescue attempt.

Q3: Don't over-penalize Stage-0 — it's ~3-way on a property with prior evidence (865 passed Probe 3). Bonferroni at α/3, not α/3000. Q4: 1298's rank-1 Sharpe is H1 evidence — rank-1 AND coherent is a joint signal. Q5: Inherit Probe 2 gate exactly — tightening is Contrarian's move.

The Outsider

Coming in cold: 1298 being rank 1 of 3000 should scare you, not excite you. You watched two ML#2 stacks that "looked statistically strong" dissolve under combo-agnostic audit last week. Why would the literal extremum be the exception? 1298's rank-1 is H0 discount, not H1 boost. 1149 at rank 4 / 101 trades is actually more honest.

Including 664 as negative control is the only option that would convince an outsider you're doing science rather than confirmation-hunting. Run D.

Stage-0 isn't "pick 1 of 3" — it's 3000-sweep multiplicity with a filter. The filter itself needs justification.

Naive question: what stops the overnight-GLOBEX concentration from being the ACTUAL signal, with combo-865 just being one of many parameter realizations that happen to trade it? If yes, B1 (session-structure sweep) should come first, not B2.

The Executor

Monday morning: Option B — 1298 + 1149 in parallel. Both pre-gate PASS, both fit in one remote job. Sequencing 1298 first buys nothing — 1h evaluation is cheap (Probe 2 harness exists). Drop 664 as negative control — doubles writeup burden; you already have 865 @ 15m from Probe 2.

Q2: B2 before B1. If 1298 or 1149 passes, B1 becomes "map the edge surface"; if both fail, B1 becomes "is 865 a true singleton." Don't write B1 twice.

Q3: Bonferroni the gate — picked best 2 of 3, inflate effective α by 2. Mechanical fix: net Sharpe ≥ 1.4 (not 1.3). Q4: Discount H1 for 1298 — rank-1 is construction extremum. Q5: Reuse gate structure but raise Sharpe to 1.4.

First concrete step: write B2 preregistration, sign, push to sweep-runner-1.
